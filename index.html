Have you ever had a party at which a lot of party pics were shot? Ever wanted to make a slideshow of those photos set to a musical background consisting of the song Right Round as featured in the hit movie The Hangover? Well now you can and it is so easy that you will be able to perform the necessary steps even when still hungover.

With this bitstarter I am trying to raise the funds necessary to create a web-app which can turn your party pics into a credit roll Hangover style. By backing this project you can help realizing this piece of software which will have a huge impact on the way people view and relive their favorite memories (if they remember them at all!).

FAQ

No this project and the web-app is intended to be open source and free of charge for users.
